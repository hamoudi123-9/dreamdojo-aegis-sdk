## 📦 What is dreamdojo-aegis-sdk?

The dreamdojo-aegis-sdk is a tool that helps check if AI models working in virtual or physical environments are safe. It watches how these AI systems act in the world and makes sure they do not cause problems or behave in risky ways. This is important when AI controls robots or other machines in real life or simulations.

The SDK is mainly designed to work with NVIDIA DreamDojo or DiT pipelines, which are advanced setups for training AI models that learn how to move and act inside digital worlds. By using this SDK, developers get a framework to keep their AI models reliable and safe during use.

---

## 🔍 Key Features

- Runtime safety checks for AI models during operation  
- Supports embodied AI models that interact with 3D environments  
- Compatible with NVIDIA DreamDojo and DiT AI pipelines  
- Designed to prevent unsafe or harmful AI behavior  
- Can be part of simulation-to-reality workflows  
- Built with Rust for speed and reliability
